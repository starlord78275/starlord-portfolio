Emotion-Based Music Player

The Emotion-Based Music Player is a unique application that uses computer vision and facial emotion recognition to adapt the music selection based on the user's current mood. By analyzing the user's facial expressions, the app suggests music genres that align with their detected emotions, enhancing the listening experience.

Key Features

  • Facial emotion recognition for personalized music suggestions
  • Real-time music adaptation based on mood changes
  • Interactive UI with seamless music integration
